Modes Hub

DeepSeek TUI modes change how aggressive the agent behaves in your terminal workflow

Mode choice matters because it changes how directly the agent takes action, how much you review first, and how comfortable the tool feels in higher-risk sessions.

Simple Framing

Plan mode is the more cautious route. Yolo mode is the faster, less hesitant route.

Neither mode is universally better. The right choice depends on your workflow and trust level.
